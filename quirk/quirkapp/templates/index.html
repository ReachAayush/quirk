No more lame playlists.

Hosting a party can leave you managing the music. No more. Let your guests add and upvote their favorite songs from their mobile phones. No app to download or subscription required.

  Join our mailing list

1. Create a Rukkus

Install Rukkus.fm from the Spotify App Finder and start a new Rukkus. Drag in a playlist to get some music started.

2. Join the Rukkus

Direct your guests to www.rukkus.fm on their mobile phones. We'll use geolocation to show them a list of nearby parties.

3. Add and upvote

Add and upvote your favorite songs. Watch as the playlist shuffles as songs compete to be played.

Rukkus.fm was developed by four students at Carnegie Mellon University as part of the TartanHacks hackathon in February of 2012. Winning Twitter's Best User Experience award, Rukkus.fm provides a no-nonsense, incredibly easy set up process, and gorgeous interface.

Letting everyone collaborate on the playlist takes the stress out of hosting a party. Powered by Spotify, party guests can pick from over 14 million songs and vote to control the playlist.

Relax and get ready to Rukkus.
